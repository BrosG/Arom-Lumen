Romain works alongside design professionals. Custom dimensions, material palettes, and bespoke forms -- crafted to your specification, guided by fifteen years of woodworking mastery.

The Partnership

Design-Led, Craft-Driven

You do not need another product catalogue. You need a craftsman who understands design intent, respects deadlines, and can translate your vision into something the factory cannot produce.

Romain has spent over a dozen years as a cabinetmaker and woodworker before dedicating himself to sculptural lighting. He speaks the language of material specifications, tolerances, and lead times. He understands that your client's project has a timeline and a budget, and he works within both while delivering work that exceeds expectation.

Material Range

The Palette

Shou Sugi Ban + Steam Bending

Douglas Fir · Ash · Elm

Controlled charring reveals deep textures. Steam bending creates organic curves impossible to machine. Combined: a sculptural language unique to Arom Lumen.

Precious Veneers + Metal

Rosewood · Teak · Walnut · Steel

Rosewood, teak, and walnut veneers diffuse light with warmth. Steel and chain add structural counterpoint. Mix and match to suit your material palette.

Lathe-Turned Hardwoods

Iroko · Sipo · Spruce · Oak

Precision turning shapes exotic and European hardwoods into seamless forms. Ideal for smaller-scale pieces and multiples.

Working Together

The Process

◆

Material Library

Over 15 wood species currently in the workshop: Douglas fir, ash, elm, oak, walnut, rosewood, teak, iroko, sipo, spruce, ebony, and more. Physical samples available upon request for your client presentations.

◆

Custom Dimensions

Current range spans 35 cm to 215 cm. Heights, widths, and shade diameters can be adapted to your specification. Share your elevation drawings and Romain will propose proportions that work.

◆

Finish Options

Natural oil, Shou Sugi Ban (light to deep char), ebonisation, hand-painted accents, raw unfinished surfaces. Each finish affects the quality of light the piece casts. Romain can advise on the best match for your lighting design intent.

◆

Lead Times & Logistics

Typical lead time: 4 to 8 weeks per piece depending on complexity. Multi-piece commissions are phased and scheduled to align with your project timeline. Worldwide shipping arranged, with custom crating for safe delivery.

◆

Lighting Specifications

Warm white LED strips and bulbs, dimmable as standard. Colour temperature, lumen output, and driver specifications provided for your lighting schedules. Compatible with most dimming systems.
