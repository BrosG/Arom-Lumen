The Perspective

Sculpture, Not Furniture

These are not lamps displayed as art. They are sculptures that happen to produce light. The medium is wood, fire, and time. The result is something that belongs in a gallery as much as it belongs in a home.

Romain Davidico approaches each creation as a sculptor first. The wood is selected for its character. The fire is applied as an artistic gesture -- Shou Sugi Ban, an ancient Japanese technique, reveals hidden topographies in the grain. Steam bending transforms rigid timber into flowing curves. Light is the final material, the soul that brings each piece to life.

Collectible Originals

The Most Sculptural Pieces

Sculpture Brûlée

35 cm · American Elm · Shou Sugi Ban

Each carbonised facet reveals a unique topography. A miniature landscape born of fire -- the most purely sculptural piece in the collection.

Courbe Ébène

150 cm · Steam-Bent Ash · Ebonised Oak

A gesture suspended between earth and light. The steam-bent ash curve is a line drawn in three-dimensional space -- sculpture as much as luminaire.

Majesté

215 cm · Douglas Fir · Ash · Elm

Over two metres of vertical presence. Three wood species, two techniques, one unified vision. The centrepiece of any exhibition.

Exhibition Contexts

Where Art Meets Audience

◆

Gallery Exhibitions

Solo or group shows featuring the full range of Arom Lumen creations. Each piece is displayed as sculpture, with its material story, technique, and artistic intention presented alongside.

◆

Art Fairs & Salons

International design fairs and craft salons where collectors discover new voices. Arom Lumen pieces attract attention for their scale, materiality, and the unmistakable mark of the human hand.

◆

Private Collections

For the collector who seeks functional art. Each piece is a signed original, accompanied by a certificate of authenticity documenting the wood species, techniques, and the story of its creation.

◆

Museum Gift Shops

Smaller pieces like the Sculpture Brulee appeal to museum visitors seeking authentic, handcrafted art objects at an accessible price point -- artisanal, collectible, and deeply personal.
